Laker's Legend.

1978 - 2020

Kobe Bryant was an American professional basketball player born on August 23, 1978, in Philadelphia. He spent his entire 20-year career with the Los Angeles Lakers, winning five NBA championships and becoming one of the most iconic figures in the sport's history.

He was an 18-time All-Star, two-time Olympic gold medalist, and a league MVP. He was also known for his work ethic, mental toughness, and fierce competitive spirit.

Off the court, he was an Academy Award-winning producer, businessman, devoted father and husband and philanthropist.

Tragically, Kobe Bryant died in a helicopter crash on January 26, 2020, along with his daughter Gianna and seven others.
